Why Madagascar?

Why Madagascar?

Madagascar is home to over 150,000 species you won’t find anywhere else on the planet—but 90% of its forests have been destroyed.

With your support, we’re reviving Madagascar’s rich biodiversity by planting mangroves and other native species to bring its forests back from the brink.
